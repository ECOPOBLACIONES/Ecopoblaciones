BIENVENIDOS

Bienvenidos a Ecopoblaciones, esta es la puerta hacia la transición personal y colectiva de tu comunidad a una relación armónica y sustentable con la Tierra.

Te proporcionamos las herramientas y la asesoría para equilibrar tu relación con la naturaleza e involucrarte en el gran paso hacia la nueva humanidad.

Apoyamos y acompañamos la cocreación de comunidades de paz, sustentables y resilientes.

LA MISION

We are a team of professional friends in environmental management, communication, bioconstruction, permaculture, art, ecotourism and the healing arts. We are convinced and committed to the regeneration of nature driven by the internal transition of human beings to a harmonious and biocentric relationship with Mother Earth.

NUESTROS PATROCINADORES

OUR STORY

We work in Ecuador in 3 projects, one in Manabí, at the rural community of Rambuche, in a Ecosocial Regenerative Post Earthquake program program dedicated to help this community to rebuild and restore their houses, farms and nature in a sustainable, resilient and peacefull way, through implementing a Permaculture Communitary Center
